A weekly check-in for your pet

How are they doing?

1.Scan the code with your phone's camera and open the link.

2.Add your pet, then take a few quiet minutes each week to score seven simple things — comfort, appetite, hydration, hygiene, happiness, mobility, and whether good days outnumbered bad.

3.Jot notes on anything worth remembering. Before your next appointment, tap "Prepare vet visit summary" and bring it with you.

Tip:Use "Add to Home Screen" in your browser so it opens like an app, and use Export now and then to back up your entries — everything is stored privately on your own phone.

This tracker is an observation aid to support conversations with your veterinarian — it is not medical advice. Your entries never leave your device.
